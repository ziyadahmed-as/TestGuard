Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take an exam?

Navigate to the "My Exams" section, select the exam you want to take, and follow the on-screen instructions. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and allow camera access if required.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

Try refreshing your browser first. If the issue persists, contact your instructor or our support team with details about the problem.

How does the proctoring system work?

Our system monitors exam sessions for unusual activity using AI algorithms. Instructors receive alerts for potential violations which they can review after the exam.
